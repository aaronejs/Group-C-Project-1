How to apply for the dance course:

1. Go to studielink.nl

2. If you already have an account on Studielink,

then choose to login with your preferred method.

If you do not own a Studielink account then choose

to create an account without a DigiD

3. Click on 'Study programmes'


4. Click 'Add new enrolment application'


5. In 'Educational institution',

pick 'NHL Stenden University of Applied Sciences'


6. Scroll through the list until you find

'B INTERNATIONAL DANCE COURSE'

7. Select the course and fill all the required information.

Good luck!
